About us

We are data privacy and security researchers from TCS Research, India.

As part of our research, we have come up with this patented interface of Pass-O, where users are influenced to choose and remember relatively difficult text-based passwords with minimal additional cognitive load.

To know more about our research on Pass-O, please visit our recently published poster here at SOUPS 2016 conference.

You are encouraged to use Pass-O, and provide your valuable feedback to us.
